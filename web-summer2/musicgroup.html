Musical groups


You can be a part of a musical group no matter what age you are. There are a few types of groups you can be a part of:

  • choir(where you sing)
  • band(where you play instruments such as the trumpet, saxophone, flute and clarinet. You can also have a band where you play and sing together such as the backstreet boys and DAY6)
  • orchestra(where You play instruments such as the violin, the viola, the cello, and the bass)
