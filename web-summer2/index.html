Music


Music has had a tremendous impact on our lives. Whether it's a song we really like to listen to or mastering an instrument, music can change the way someone feels, and can even change the way someone thinks. It can make you feel happy or sad, and can make you want to dance. Music can bring people all across the world together.

Origins


Music has been around for over 40,000 years. Research suggests it may have allowed our distant ancestors to communicate before the invention of language. It helped provide the social glue needed for the emergence of the first pre-human societies.
